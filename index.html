A Dedicated Vision

The Art of Piano Playing
for Young Pupils

For a young pianist, the first steps into the professional world are the most critical. It is not just about learning notes; it is about discovering a voice.

We believe that the time spent with a master should be a revelation. By focusing our energy into intensive, high-impact sessions, we honor the young student's natural curiosity and agility, ensuring every minute at the keyboard is a step toward artistic independence.

1

A Personal Connection

Every child is unique. Our 30-minute modules are intense but deeply human, allowing us to connect with the student's specific musical personality and build their confidence from the very first minute.

2

The Path to the Stage

We don't just teach pieces; we prepare performers. Through four iterative touchpoints, we refine not only the technique but the presence and focus required for a professional solo recital.

"Our mission is to inspire and support young pianists by giving them an international stage to grow and push their limits."

Philippe Raskin

Artistic Director & Professor

Philippe Raskin’s teaching is a dialogue. He focuses on helping young students understand the "why" behind every note, fostering a deep intellectual and emotional connection to the keyboard.

Cristiana Dragos

Professor & Artistic Coordinator

Cristiana specializes in the delicate transition from student to artist. Her approach is nurturing yet rigorous, ensuring every young pupil leaves with a clear path for their future development.
